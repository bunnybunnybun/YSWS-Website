Oojamaflip

Make your first desktop application, get free computer peripherals (Keyboard, mouse, macropad etc.)!

There will be guides to help you get started, in both regular article-like format for individual participants, and another version in slideshow format for clubs to host workshops!

Rewards

Rewards are based on how much time you work on your project for!
For 5-10 hours: $20 computer peripherals grant and stickers.
For 10+ hours: $40 computer peripherals grant and stickers.

FAQ

Who can participate in this?

Anyone aged 13 through 18 (inclusive)!

What do I have to do to get the free stuff?!?

Code a project in Python, and "ship" it! "Shipping" means submitting your project to the event. To submit, you must upload your project, show us how much time you spent on it using Hackatime, and be under 19 years old.

I've never coded before, can I still participate?

Yes! This program is specificaly made for people with little to no prior experience, you are more than welcome to join!

Is this real? It seems to good to be true.

(Not yet haha this is just a draft website 😜) Yep! It's completely free, and you do not give up ownership of the code you submit to the event, it's still yours! This event is made for teenagers, by teenagers from Hack Club, a community of over 100k teens from all over the world that run events like this all the time!
